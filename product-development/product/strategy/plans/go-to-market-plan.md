# Go-To-Market Plan

## Objective

Launch JetLag with a low-cost, AI-augmented marketing engine that can create awareness, capture demand, and convert early travelers into paying users.

## Four-Phase Marketing Plan

### 1. Positioning And Foundations

- Clarify value proposition and competitor comparison
- Build launch messaging for `Free`, `Core`, and `Premium`
- Prepare landing pages, email capture, and core content assets

### 2. Organic Demand Capture

- Publish travel-performance and jet lag education content
- Respond to real discussions surfaced by the daily opportunity scanner
- Create comparison content against Timeshifter, StopJetLag, and Rooster

### 3. Conversion Optimization

- Test onboarding, pricing presentation, and paywall framing
- Improve signup to first-plan conversion
- Identify the strongest conversion channel mix

### 4. Retention And Expansion

- Drive repeat-trip usage
- Collect testimonials and proof points
- Explore referrals, partnerships, and premium upsell moments
