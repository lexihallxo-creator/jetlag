# 90-Day Launch Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation

- Finalize product positioning and pricing across `Free`, `Core`, and `Premium`
- Stand up the no-investment or low-cost tech stack
- Lock the onboarding flow for trip entry and first-plan generation
- Define baseline KPI dashboard and operating review cadence

## Phase 2: Product Readiness

- Ship itinerary capture and personalized recovery-plan generation
- Launch reminder logic for pre-trip, travel-day, and arrival-day actions
- Instrument the activation funnel and first-trip engagement events
- Validate end-to-end user flow with live testers

## Phase 3: Launch Engine

- Execute the initial marketing plan across owned content, community, and organic engagement
- Use the daily opportunity scanner to find relevant discussions and response opportunities every morning
- Publish comparison content against key competitors
- Begin collecting testimonials, objections, and conversion insights

## Phase 4: Optimization

- Review pricing conversion across plan tiers
- Improve onboarding drop-off points
- Tune reminder timing and plan clarity from real-user feedback
- Prioritize retention work around second-trip usage

## Success Signals

- Consistent daily top-of-funnel opportunities from community scanning
- Healthy conversion from free users into `Core`
- Evidence that users complete recommended actions during their first trip
- Clear path toward the Year 1 revenue target
