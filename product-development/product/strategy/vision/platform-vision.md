# JetLag Vision

JetLag should become the most trusted travel companion for managing time-zone recovery.

In the near term, the company wins by helping travelers get a personalized plan fast and follow it with confidence. In the longer term, JetLag can become the operating layer for travel readiness, combining recovery planning, behavioral guidance, and partner distribution across travel, wellness, and employer channels.

## Product Principles

- Start with immediate traveler outcomes, not generic education.
- Make credible science feel easy to use.
- Keep the interface lightweight enough to support travel reality.
- Use automation to scale business operations without making the product feel robotic.

## Company Principles

- Design the company so AI can handle most repeatable work.
- Keep fixed costs low and use no-code or low-cost tools where possible.
- Build for trust, retention, and repeat-trip value before expanding breadth.
