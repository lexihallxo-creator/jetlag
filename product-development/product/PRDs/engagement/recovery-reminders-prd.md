# PRD: Recovery Reminders

## Problem

Even a strong plan fails if the user forgets to follow it during travel.

## Goal

Send timely reminders that help travelers act on the plan without making the product feel noisy.

## Scope

- Pre-trip reminders
- Travel-day reminders
- Arrival-day reminders
- Basic user controls for notification timing and intensity

## Success Metrics

- Reminder open rate
- Action completion after reminder delivery
- Notification opt-out rate
