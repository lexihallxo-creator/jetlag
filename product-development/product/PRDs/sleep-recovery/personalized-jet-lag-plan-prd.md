# PRD: Personalized Jet Lag Plan

## Problem

Generic jet lag advice is easy to ignore because it does not feel tailored to the actual trip.

## Goal

Generate a clear, personalized recovery plan tied to the user's itinerary and intended sleep schedule.

## Scope

- Pre-departure guidance
- Travel-day guidance
- First 72 hours after arrival
- Lightweight explanation of why the plan is recommended

## Success Metrics

- Plan generation rate
- First-day action completion
- Self-reported usefulness
