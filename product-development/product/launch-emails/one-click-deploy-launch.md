## Introducing One-Click Deploy

Starting today, every Forge project includes a **Deploy** button that takes your app from prototype to production in under 5 minutes.

### How it works

1. **Click Deploy** in any Forge project
2. **Choose your target** - AWS, Google Cloud, or Vercel
3. **Configure your environment** - region, environment variables, custom domains
4. **Review and confirm** - we show you exactly what infrastructure will be created
5. **You're live** - your app gets a production URL, SSL certificate, and monitoring dashboard

### What's included

- **Infrastructure as Code** - every deployment generates clean Terraform configurations you own
- **Auto-scaling** - your app scales based on traffic automatically
- **Rollback** - one-click rollback to any previous deployment version
- **Environment management** - staging and production environments with promotion workflows
- **Custom domains** - connect your own domain with automatic SSL provisioning
- **Monitoring** - built-in health checks, uptime monitoring, and error alerting

## Getting Started

One-Click Deploy is available today for all Growth and Enterprise customers. To get started:

1. Open any Forge project
2. Click the new **Deploy** button in the top toolbar
3. Follow the guided setup (takes about 2 minutes for first-time configuration)

We've also published a [deployment guide](https://docs.forgelabs.dev/deploy) with walkthroughs for each cloud provider and advanced configuration options.

**Starter plan customers:** One-Click Deploy will be available on Starter plans beginning May 15. You can [upgrade today](https://app.forgelabs.dev/billing) to get immediate access.
