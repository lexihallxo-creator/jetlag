# Bolt — TL;DR

## Overview
Full-stack AI app builder at bolt.new. Speed-focused — prompt to deployed app as fast as possible.

## Key Strengths
- Fast generation with good UI quality
- One-click deployment with hosting included
- Iteration without full regeneration
- Multi-page app generation
- Simple, approachable UX for non-technical users

## Key Weaknesses
- No enterprise features (no SSO, audit logs, compliance)
- Limited collaboration (no multiplayer, basic team workspaces)
- No CI/CD pipeline
- Limited customization (no architecture pattern selection, no plugin system)
- Partial integrations (basic Git, basic database connectors)

## ICP Overlap
Solo founders, small teams, non-technical builders. Similar to Lovable's target but more speed-focused. Competes for the "just build it fast" use case.

## Pricing
Freemium with usage-based tiers. Starting at ~$20/mo.

## Forge Differentiation
Forge wins on enterprise features, customization depth, and production-readiness. Bolt wins on speed and simplicity for simple apps. Similar dynamic to Lovable but Bolt is more full-stack.

## Bottom Line
Direct competitor for the SMB segment. Strong on speed and simplicity, weak on enterprise and customization. Growing fast — watch their feature velocity.
