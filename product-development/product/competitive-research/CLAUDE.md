# Competitive Research

Competitive intelligence for JetLag.

## Primary Competitors

| Competitor | Focus | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Timeshifter | Science-backed jet lag planning app | Strong category benchmark and likely trust leader |
| StopJetLag | Jet lag mitigation planning | Competes on direct recovery use case |
| Rooster | Time-zone adjustment and sleep scheduling | Relevant alternative in traveler decision set |

## Positioning Questions

- How does JetLag feel simpler or more actionable than existing tools?
- Where can JetLag win on clarity, onboarding speed, pricing, or reminder quality?
- Which competitor objections appear most often in customer conversations and community posts?

## Recommended Research Assets

- Pricing comparison
- Feature and onboarding comparison
- Trust and science-language comparison
- Community sentiment snapshots from Reddit, Quora, and LinkedIn

## Update Triggers

- Major competitor pricing or feature changes
- New user research mentioning alternatives
- Conversion friction linked to competitive confusion
