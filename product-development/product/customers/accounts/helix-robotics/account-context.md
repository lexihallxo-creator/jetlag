# Helix Robotics - Account Context

## What They Use Forge For

Robotics company with a 15-person team. Uses Forge for web application prototyping related to their hardware products. Running 11 active projects.

## Goals and Success Criteria

- Expand Forge usage to firmware prototyping use cases
- Python/FastAPI framework support (currently limited to React/Next.js)
- Hardware integration templates

## Relationship Context

- **Champion:** Rachel Torres (Director of Engineering) - technically deep, provides strong competitive feedback

## Open Risks and Blockers

- No immediate churn risk - satisfied with current web prototyping use case
- Interested in multi-language support (Python, Go)
