Axiom Logistics is a national logistics and supply chain company with a 28-person engineering team. The rate limiting fix — including a dedicated capacity tier and a monitoring dashboard — shipped on March 20.

## Key Themes

### 1. Rate Limiting Resolution (Positive — Resolved)

The dedicated capacity tier shipped on March 20 and Deepa confirmed the rate limiting issue is fully resolved. Zero 429 errors in the 4 days since the fix went live, even during the 2-4 PM peak window. What impressed Deepa even more than the fix itself was the monitoring dashboard that shipped alongside it: "I have to be honest — after the rate limiting issues, my team was ready to evaluate alternatives. But the speed of your response completely turned that around. You didn't just fix the bug, you shipped a monitoring dashboard so we can see it ourselves. That's the kind of partnership that keeps us here." This is a significant save on an account that was at real churn risk two weeks ago.

### 2. Preview Environments for Staging (Pain Point — P1)

With rate limiting resolved, Deepa pivoted to her next priority: preview environments. Her team currently deploys directly to production with no staging step, which creates anxiety and risk. She wants a workflow where engineers deploy to a temporary staging URL, share it with stakeholders for approval, and then promote to production with one click. Deepa framed this as essential for the 4 tools that are now unblocked by the CI/CD scoping work.

### 3. Environment Variable Management (Pain Point — P1)

Axiom deploys the same internal tools across multiple configurations — different API endpoints for different regional warehouses, different auth tokens for different environments. Today this requires manually editing config files before each deploy. Deepa is requesting a UI for managing environment variables per deploy target, with the ability to save profiles for dev, staging, and production.
