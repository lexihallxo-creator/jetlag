# Acme Corp - Account Context

## What They Use Forge For

50-person B2B SaaS startup in Austin building workforce management software. Engineering team of 12 uses Forge for rapid prototyping of admin dashboards, data visualization tools, and customer-facing onboarding flows. Currently running 8 active projects with 35 generation runs per week and 3 production apps deployed last quarter.

## Goals and Success Criteria

- Accelerate internal tool development (their primary use case)
- Expand Forge usage from prototyping into production workflows
- Add 8 more seats to cover the full engineering team

## Relationship Context

- **Champion:** Derek Liu (VP Engineering) - consistently positive about generation quality, actively advocates for Forge internally
- **Power user:** Priya Nair (Lead Developer) - provides detailed technical feedback, wants shared templates and collaboration features
- **Executive sponsor:** Tom Beckett (CTO) - involved in QBRs, focused on ROI and team productivity

## Open Risks and Blockers

- Deployment friction is their biggest pain point - built 6 internal tools last quarter but only deployed 2 because of infrastructure setup overhead
- Wants collaboration features (shared templates, project visibility) - two engineers independently built nearly identical forms due to no visibility
- Renewal is June 2026 with strong expansion signal, but deployment improvements are needed to unlock full team adoption
