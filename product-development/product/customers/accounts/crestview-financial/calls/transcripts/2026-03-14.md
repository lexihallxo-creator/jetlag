## Audit Log Gaps (18:00 - 32:00)

**Hannah Stulberg:** Diana, I know you've been digging into the audit log side. Can you walk us through what you're seeing?

**Diana Kowalski:** Yes. Last week I tried to trace who deployed a financial reporting tool to production. There's no audit trail. I can see the project exists, but I can't tell you who created it, who modified it, or who deployed it. That's a SOC 2 finding we can't have.

**Riley Patel:** Can you walk me through exactly what you did? I want to understand what information you expected to find versus what was available.

**Diana Kowalski:** Sure. I went to the project dashboard, opened the reporting tool project, and looked for any kind of history or activity log. There's a "Last modified" timestamp but no user attribution. I then checked the deployment section — I can see the live URL, but there's no record of who triggered the deploy, when they triggered it, or what version was deployed. For SOC 2, I need to be able to answer: who did what, when, and what changed.

**Riley Patel:** That's really helpful, Diana. You're right that we don't currently capture deploy-level attribution or maintain a chronological activity log at the project level. This is on our Q2 roadmap. Can I ask — what format would be most useful? Would an in-app activity feed work, or do you need exportable logs?

**Diana Kowalski:** Both ideally. The activity feed is useful for day-to-day, but for audits I need CSV or JSON export with timestamps, user IDs, action types, and affected resources. And it needs to be immutable — our auditors will ask whether the logs can be tampered with.

**Alex Rivera:** Immutability is a great callout. We'll factor that into the design.

**Diana Kowalski:** One more thing — I need retention. SOC 2 requires us to retain audit logs for at least one year. What's the current retention policy?

**Riley Patel:** Honestly, since we don't have comprehensive audit logging yet, there isn't a formal retention policy. But when we build this, we'll design for at least one-year retention. I'll include retention details in the roadmap document we send over.
