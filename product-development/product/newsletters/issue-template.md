# Issue Template

## Subject Line

Use a sharp, curiosity-heavy subject line that signals specificity.

## Opening

Start with a surprising observation, mistake, or scenario.

## Why This Happens

Explain the underlying issue simply.

## What To Do Instead

Give the practical framework, sequence, or checklist.

## One Concrete Scenario

Show how the advice applies in a real trip situation.

## Close

End with one sentence that reinforces JetLag's point of view.

## CTA

Use one:

- Reply with your upcoming route and I’ll tell you the first thing I’d change.
- Forward this to someone flying soon.
- If you have a trip in the next 7 days, book a JetLag Express plan.
