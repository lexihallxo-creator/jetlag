# Publishing Workflow

## Objective

Produce a high-value newsletter consistently without falling into generic advice or founder burnout.

## Inputs

- Daily opportunity scanner output
- Customer calls or concierge plan requests
- Competitor observations
- Founder travel experience or pattern recognition
- Existing product insights from JetLag docs

## Weekly Workflow

### 1. Gather Signals

- Review daily scans for repeated pain points
- Note questions people keep asking
- Save strong traveler scenarios and emotional language

### 2. Pick One Strong Angle

Choose the angle with the highest combination of:

- usefulness
- uniqueness
- emotional relevance
- conversion potential

### 3. Draft Fast

Use `issue-template.md`.

Write the opening with a sharp observation, not a generic intro.

### 4. Add One Memorable Framework

Each issue should have a named idea, model, or checklist so it feels ownable and referable.

### 5. End With One Clear CTA

Options:

- reply with your upcoming route
- book a JetLag Express plan
- forward this to someone flying soon
- join the waitlist

## Quality Bar

Before publishing, ask:

- Would someone save this?
- Would someone forward this?
- Is this more practical than a generic blog post?
- Does it sound like JetLag, not a content mill?
