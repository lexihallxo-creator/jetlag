# JetLag Analytics

Analytics resources for JetLag's launch, growth, and retention engine.

## Core Use Cases

- KPI dashboard review
- Pricing and funnel analysis
- Reminder and engagement measurement
- Revenue tracking against launch plan
- Community opportunity reporting from daily scans

## Core KPIs

| KPI | Why It Matters |
|-----|----------------|
| Visitor to signup conversion | Measures acquisition and landing-page quality |
| Signup to first plan conversion | Measures time-to-value |
| Free to Core conversion | Primary monetization health |
| Core to Premium upgrade rate | Premium value validation |
| First-trip action completion rate | Evidence the product is helping |
| Second-trip retention | Best indicator of durable value |
| CAC by channel | Ensures the lean model stays efficient |
| Revenue vs plan | Tracks progress toward `$120K`, `$750K`, and `$1.2M ARR` goals |

## Reporting Cadence

- Daily: top-of-funnel opportunity scan and engagement ideas
- Weekly: activation, conversion, and support review
- Monthly: pricing, retention, and revenue review

## Data Inputs

- Product analytics events
- Subscription and billing data
- Marketing channel performance
- Daily opportunity scanner outputs in the project `daily-scans` folder
