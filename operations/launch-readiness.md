# Launch Readiness

## What Exists Now

- A concierge sales landing page
- A revenue sprint playbook
- Outreach scripts
- Client intake and delivery templates
- Newsletter strategy, formats, and first issue draft
- Daily revenue scan automation
- Repo and documentation structure for JetLag

## What Still Needs To Be Wired

### Required To Start Taking Revenue

- A live payment link
- A deployed public page
- A simple intake handoff after payment

### Required To Start Newsletter Growth

- A newsletter platform or email collection tool
- A real signup form or waitlist destination
- A sending workflow from your email tool

### Required To Collect Feedback Reliably

- A lightweight feedback form
- A place to store responses
- A basic tagging system for pain points and objections

## Fastest Stack

- Website: publish the existing HTML page with GitHub Pages, Carrd, Framer, or Typedream
- Payments: Stripe Payment Link or Gumroad
- Newsletter: Beehiiv, ConvertKit, or Substack
- Feedback: Tally or Google Form
- Tracking: manual dashboard plus spreadsheet at first

## Minimum Viable Launch

You can start today if you wire these four links into the website:

1. `Book now`
2. `Pay now`
3. `Subscribe`
4. `Send feedback`

## Recommendation

Do not wait for a full app.

Launch the concierge offer, collect emails, collect traveler pain points, and use that demand to shape the product.
