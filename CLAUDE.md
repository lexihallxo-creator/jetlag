# JetLag

## Operating Model

JetLag is being built as an AI-first company designed to let one operator run a multi-function business and scale toward a five-department workforce with AI handling most repeatable work.

## Departments

| Department | Scope |
|------------|-------|
| Product and Engineering | Product strategy, requirements, build planning, implementation, QA |
| Marketing and Growth | Positioning, content, launch campaigns, distribution, partnerships |
| Customer Success | Traveler support, onboarding help, feedback loops, retention |
| Finance and Operations | Pricing, forecasting, KPI review, operating cadence |
| Organic Community Engagement | Daily opportunity scanning, social listening, response drafts, community learning |

## Working Rules

- Check `product-development/feature-index.yaml` first when looking up work for a feature
- Keep business context, pricing, and growth strategy in `product-development/product/strategy/`
- Keep product requirements in `product-development/product/PRDs/`
- Keep engineering execution in `product-development/engineering/plans/`
- Keep KPI definitions and reporting artifacts in `product-development/analytics/`
- Use `team/` for operating-model and onboarding docs
