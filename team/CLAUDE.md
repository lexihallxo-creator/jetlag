# Team

JetLag is operating with a lean AI-first workforce design.

## Workforce Model

The business is designed so AI handles 80% or more of recurring work while one person can coordinate strategy, approvals, and high-leverage decisions. As the company scales, work expands across five functional departments.

## Departments

| Department | Examples of Work |
|------------|------------------|
| Product and Engineering | Roadmap, specs, build plans, releases, QA |
| Marketing and Growth | Messaging, content, campaigns, SEO, partnerships |
| Customer Success | Support workflows, user education, retention feedback |
| Finance and Operations | Forecasting, KPI review, pricing analysis, process |
| Organic Community Engagement | Social listening, response drafts, trend detection, community posting |

## Operating Rhythm

- Daily: review opportunity scanner output and high-priority growth or support actions
- Weekly: review product progress, funnel metrics, and user feedback
- Monthly: review revenue, retention, pricing performance, and roadmap shifts

## Onboarding Guides

The role-based onboarding guides in `onboarding-guides/` can be repurposed into AI playbooks or future team onboarding as JetLag scales.
