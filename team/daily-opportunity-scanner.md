# Daily Opportunity Scanner

## Purpose

The Daily Opportunity Scanner runs each morning at `8:04 AM` and looks for relevant jet lag, travel fatigue, sleep, and international travel discussions that JetLag can learn from or engage with.

## Sources

- Reddit communities such as `r/travel`, `r/digitalnomad`, `r/biohacking`, and `r/sleep`
- Quora discussions
- LinkedIn conversations

## Expected Output

Each daily report should include:

- High-priority engagement opportunities
- Trending topics
- Content ideas
- Draft responses

## Output Location

Reports should be saved in the project `daily-scans` folder.

## Why It Matters

This scanner is a core part of the Organic Community Engagement function and gives JetLag a repeatable way to find demand, sharpen messaging, and identify user pain points without paying for heavy outbound acquisition.
