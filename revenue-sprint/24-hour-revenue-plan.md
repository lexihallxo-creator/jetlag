# 24-Hour Revenue Plan

## Objective

Make the first sale for JetLag by selling a manual concierge jet lag recovery plan before the full product is built.

## What We Need

- A clear paid offer
- A simple landing page or checkout page
- Outreach messages for people with upcoming international trips
- A short intake form
- A fast delivery template
- A same-day follow-up process

## Offer To Sell Today

Sell a personalized `JetLag Express Plan` for travelers leaving soon.

### Suggested Starting Price

- `$19` for first 3 customers if speed matters most
- `$29` if you want the price to match the future `Core` product more closely
- Optional upsell: `$49` for rush turnaround or extra trip support

## Ideal First Customers

- Friends or friends-of-friends flying internationally this week
- Business travelers posting about long-haul travel
- Digital nomads planning relocations or multi-time-zone trips
- Reddit and LinkedIn users actively complaining about jet lag or sleep disruption

## Execution Order

### Hour 1

- Pick the starting price
- Publish the landing page copy somewhere usable
- Create a payment link with Stripe, Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy, or another fast tool

### Hours 2-4

- Send direct messages to warm contacts first
- Post in founder, traveler, and nomad circles
- Reply to high-intent community threads where people are asking for help

### Hours 4-8

- Take the first responses into DMs
- Send payment link
- Immediately send intake questions after payment

### Hours 8-24

- Deliver the plan fast
- Ask for a testimonial
- Use the testimonial to close the next customers

## Non-Negotiables

- Sell the outcome, not the future app
- Focus on people traveling in the next 7 days
- Keep the offer narrow and easy to buy
- Deliver fast enough that customers feel momentum immediately
