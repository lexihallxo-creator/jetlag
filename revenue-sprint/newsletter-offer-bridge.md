# Newsletter To Revenue Bridge

## Why This Exists

The newsletter should not just build audience. It should also create natural entry points into paid JetLag offers.

## Soft CTAs

- Reply with your upcoming route
- Forward this to someone flying soon
- Tell me where jet lag usually destroys your trip

## Revenue CTAs

- Book a JetLag Express plan for your next trip
- Get a personalized recovery plan before you fly
- Reply `PLAN` if you want me to build your schedule manually

## Best Rule

Earn trust first, then ask for the sale.

Most issues should be about value. Some should convert. All should make JetLag feel more credible.
