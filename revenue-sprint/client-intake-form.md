# Client Intake Form

Send these questions immediately after payment.

## Required Questions

1. What airport are you leaving from and when do you depart?
2. What airport are you arriving at and when do you land?
3. How many time zones are you crossing, if known?
4. What is your normal sleep schedule at home?
5. What do you need to feel good for after arrival: work, meetings, sightseeing, athletic performance, or general energy?
6. Do you use caffeine regularly? If yes, roughly how much?
7. Are there any important constraints: children, medication, insomnia, anxiety, overnight layovers, very short trip, or schedule immovable events?

## Optional Questions

1. Have you tried any jet lag tools before?
2. What usually goes wrong for you after long flights?
3. Do you want the plan to be aggressive or easier to follow?

## Internal Notes To Capture

- Time-zone shift magnitude
- Trip urgency
- Customer travel goal
- Any obvious risk or edge case
